Companies That Uses These Technology


Buying a home in a competitive market is a mentally taxing process that involves fourteen distinct emotional stages, according to reports. That means real estate agents must demonstrate empathy and compassion when helping potential buyers. In other words, they can't be bots. In 2014, an AI-powered bot passed the Turing Test as a 13 year boy, demonstrating enough humanity to pass. Real estate is not typically sold by thirteen year-olds. Nonetheless, artificial intelligence has revolutionized the real estate industry, which now encompasses far more than just agencies and traffic in the world's largest asset class. (The housing market in the United States alone is worth $16 trillion.) AI has changed the day-to-day work of appraisers, mortgage lenders, and developers in fundamental ways.


We've compiled a list of 7 companies that are using bots and AI-powered algorithms to revolutionize the real estate industry.
